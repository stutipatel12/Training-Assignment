FAQ

How are projects chosen for Volunteer Oveseas?

Volunteer Overseas partners with reputable organizations worldwide that offer meaniningful volunteer, intern, and teach abroad trips. We want to make sure that any program you sign up for is well-organized, safe, and impactful. A few things we look for in our partner organizations include:

- History and leadership

- Awards, accreditations, and accolades

- Participant safely and support

- Consistently positive participant reviews

- Demonstrated track record of community engagement, sustainable program management, and program improvement

- Above-and-beyond extras, such as travel insurance, cultural immersion activities, and alumni engagement oppurtunities
