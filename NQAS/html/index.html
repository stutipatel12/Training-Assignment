About NQAS

NQAS design, engineer, manufacture and supply replacement after market sugar cane harvester parts globally.Known for quality products and competitve pricing.NQAS are a leading after market spare parts challenger to CASE IH and Jonn Deere and related dealer networks.

Proudly Australian owned with more than 40 years experience and 60 staff located in four continents, NQAS deliver excellent service and exceptional products to agricultural regions throughout Oceania, Africa, Asia, North America,Central America and South America.NQAS supply 10,000 product lines to over 6,000 cutomers and 2,000 suppliers worldwide.
