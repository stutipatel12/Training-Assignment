ABOUT US

We are all Vermonters.

Suicide effects everyone, so it’s going to take everyone to face the issue of suicide in Vermont. Facing Suicide VT is a statewide prevention effort providing access to suicide prevention, education, support, and advocacy resources. By sharing our stories and talking about suicide in our state, we can start to make it easier to ask for help and get help when we need it. We can help ourselves and each other when we start facing suicide in Vermont together.

BY THE NUMBERS

Suicide is the second leading cause of death among Vermonters ages 15 to 34.
