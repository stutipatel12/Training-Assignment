Some people find the following things
helpful when having thoughts of suicide:

Call or text someone

• Let them know you’re having a difficult day
• Ask if they can spend some time with you

Make your space safe

• Lock up or ask someone to store potentially dangerous household items
• Create safety plan

Use coping skills

• Relaxation techniques
• Exercise
• Deep breathing

Find distractions

• Go outside
• Write about a happy memory
• Find more examples: suicidepreventionlifeline.org

If you don’t feel you can keep yourself safe right now,
seek immediate help, call 911, or the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline
